### TestersPilot privacy policy (Android-only)
**App name:** TestersPilot  
**Platform:** **Android only** (no iOS release)  

This Privacy Policy explains how TestersPilot (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the TestersPilot Android application (the “App”).

---

### Information we collect
#### Account information
If you sign in with Google, we receive and store:
- Google profile information provided during sign-in (such as **name**, **email address**, and **profile photo**, if available)
- A unique account identifier (Firebase **UID**)

#### User content and activity (community/testing features)
We store information you choose to create or submit in the App, such as:
- Your profile details shown in the App (for example display name, avatar URL)
- App listings you submit (for example app name, developer name, package name, Play Store link, description)
- Feedback/comments you post and your interactions (for example likes/dislikes)
- Participation in testing sessions (for example session status/logs needed for rewards and moderation)

#### Device information (anti-abuse / integrity)
To help prevent abuse (e.g., duplicate accounts, ban evasion, cheating), the App generates and stores a **device fingerprint**. This fingerprint is a **hashed identifier** created from certain device/build attributes available on Android (for example manufacturer/model/brand/hardware and build identifiers). We use it for safety and enforcement; it is not used for advertising.

#### Installed app check (package visibility)
The App may check whether a specific app is installed on your device (by its **package name**) so it can:
- Show “OPEN APP” vs “INSTALL APP” in testing tasks
- Launch the target app for you (or open its Google Play listing if not installed)

The App does **not** continuously scan your installed apps list for analytics; it performs checks only to support the testing flow for the package name involved.

#### Images you choose to upload
If you choose an image (e.g., a profile avatar or an app icon), the App accesses the image you select and uploads it to our image hosting provider. We store the resulting image URL in our database so the image can be displayed in the App.

---

### How we use information
We use information to:
- Provide core functionality (sign-in, profiles, app listings, testing sessions, feedback)
- Maintain safety and integrity (fraud/abuse prevention, moderation, ban enforcement)
- Operate and improve the App (e.g., reliability and troubleshooting)

---

### How we share information
We share information only as needed to operate the App:
- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore) to sign you in and store App data
- **Google Sign-In** to enable sign-in with your Google account
- **Cloudinary** to host and deliver user-uploaded images (avatars/app icons)

#### Public/user-visible content
Some information you choose to publish is visible to other users, such as app listings, listing icons, and feedback text.

We do **not** sell personal data.

---

### External links
The App may open external links (for example Google Play links or community links) using Android’s external browser or other apps. We do not control those third-party sites/apps and their privacy practices.

---

### Data retention
We keep information for as long as needed to:
- Provide the App and maintain accounts
- Enforce safety policies and prevent abuse (including retaining limited records related to bans and fraud prevention where necessary)
- Comply with legal obligations
