Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the TestersPilot app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom of your profile
  4. Tap "Delete Account" (red button above Sign Out)
  5. Tap "DELETE" to confirm
  6. Re-authenticate with your Google account
  7. Your account is permanently deleted immediately

What Gets Deleted Immediately

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • All test sessions and testing history
  • All test logs and verification records
  • Your points, diamonds, and badge level
  • All apps you submitted for testing
  • Diamond transaction history
  • Your Google Sign-In account link

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your Firebase Auth account will be completely removed.

What Is Retained (Up to 90 Days)

To prevent abuse, enforcement data is kept for 90 days:

  • Ban and strike records (no personal identifiers except email)
  • Used only to prevent rule circumvention via re-registration
  • Automatically deleted after 90 days

ℹ️ If you re-register with the same email, your strikes and ban status will be restored to maintain platform integrity.
